A simple, social way to save

1) Form your Squad

It's easier to hit your goals when you're all in it together, motivating each other to hit your goals

2) Set your Squad Goal

This can be a common Squad goal or a collection of individual goals that you work towards in your Squad.

3) Save as you Spend

Every time you spend, we put a small amount aside into your savings pot. Friends can like spends to influence how much you save.

World Class Security

Certified Bank-Level Security

Squad uses state-of-the-art security practices, including the same end-to-end 256-bit SSL encryption to match the worlds leading banks. Your credentials are never stored on our servers & all sensitive data is encrypted.

Trusted Data Management

We only have access to your data in read-only mode. Nobody can touch your money. We have partnered with TrueLayer to help us aggregate your financial data without having to handle sensitive credentials ourselves.

Your Squad savings account

The money Squad saves for you is held in an instant-access account in your name at our partners Barclays.
