Our Mission

Money is an important enabler, inherent in most social interactions. Peer pressure makes it difficult to stick to your budget and paying and splitting expenses is awkward, tedious and inconsistent.

To make money social by building a virtual environment where people strive to achieve their ideal financial lives

The evolution of value exchange from bartering & cash to a cardless society has increased financial transparency. Technology presents a unique opportunity to create a new way to learn from each other, motivate each other and build a more financially stable society.

At Squad, we believe it doesn't have to be so. By breaking the taboo of money and creating mechanisms for friends to quickly and easily split costs as well as share their financial lives, we will revolutionise the way you approach your finances.

Our Product

Messaging lies at the heart of social interaction. Log on to Squad to enjoy group chats that can be in private Squads with friends, family or work colleagues, or public Squads with strangers about a shared interest, with professionals who can provide advice or with influencers speaking on topical issues.

Split the bill efficiently and easily on your Squad chat by using our built in P2P mobile payments system.

We give you a wholistic view of your finances and the ability to find out how your friends are spending, saving and budgeting their money. In return, you can share as much of your own data as you feel comfortable. We won't force you, but you might find it fun.
