Our Mission

To create a safe space for people to talk about their goals. We’re starting with money, the last taboo.

We want Squad to be the go to place for conversations on life goals - whether that’s saving to go travelling or walking / biking rather than taking a cab - we believe in the power of doing that as a squad, not alone.

Our Product

A message based platform where you can learn from, share and compete with your friends on spending and saving. Challenge your friends to spend less on coffees, save up for that group holiday or motivate each other to stick to that budget.

Over time, we’ll connect you to experts who can provide a helping hand when you need it - whether it’s buying your first house or paying down that credit card bill.
