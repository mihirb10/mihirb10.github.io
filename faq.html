FAQs

Why use Squad?

Simply put, saving is hard and money management is boring. Everything is more fun when we do it with our friends ad we believe saving for shared experiences should be no different. Current banking products don’t enable us to save together & motivate each other towards our goals. Squad’s group messaging app makes it easy to save, stay on track and achieve those experiences you’ve dreamed of.

----------

How does Squad work?

Squad securely analyses your transaction data in read-only mode. That means it can only view your encrypted transactions. Squad then rounds up each transaction and transferring small fixed amounts to your savings pot to ensure you reach your goal.

----------

Do I have to spend my savings on Squad rewards & deals?

No. When we do our best to provide you with great deals on unique experiences, our priority is helping you save for your dreams, whatever they may be. You’re free to withdraw & spend your hard earned savings anytime you want.

----------

Where are my Squad savings held?

Your Squad savings account is created and administered by our partner MangoPay, an EU-licensed financial institution, and your savings are held at Barclays.

----------

How safe is Squad?

Squad is extremely safe and secure. We use bank-level encryption and security practices and Squad is a read-only service so no-one can ever move money in or out of your accounts.

----------

Can I withdraw my money at any time?

Absolutely. You can instruct us to transfer back money to your regular account at any time, and it'll be there on the next business day. Of course if you do so, you may not unlock an incredible experience…

----------

Could Squad cause me to go into my overdraft?

No, we use a number of safeguards specifically designed to prevent Squad savings causing you to go into your overdraft. We hate overdraft fees too! However, if you want to save while in your overdraft, let us know at info@joinsquad.io
